EcoZero — AI Carbon Offset Platform

Predict. Offset. Net Zero. — Connecting industries to NGOs for transparent carbon neutrality.

AI Emission Prediction

Machine learning models analyse production data, energy usage, and materials to accurately predict CO₂ emissions and calculate the exact number of trees required for offset.

Carbon Target Forecasting

AI-driven trend analysis forecasts cumulative CO₂ offset over 5 years, projecting the estimated year each industry will reach carbon neutrality.

AI Planting Chatbot

An intelligent chatbot guides planters with tree growth advice, species recommendations, and soil suitability — helping NGOs make data-driven planting decisions.

Geo-Verified Tree Planting

Every planted tree is geo-tagged with coordinates and photographic evidence. Admin verification ensures full traceability on an interactive map and 3D globe.

Industry ↔ NGO Collaboration

Industries submit emissions data and fund offsets; NGOs receive planting tasks, assign planters, and verify progress — creating a transparent, end-to-end carbon credit lifecycle.

Public Transparency Dashboard

A public-facing dashboard with real-time metrics on emissions offset, trees planted, and net-zero progress. Full visibility for stakeholders and the public.
